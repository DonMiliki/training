A Brief History About Donmiliki Ventures

Donmiliki Ventures is an NGO formed to teach young school leavers how to make money through agriculture.

It was a dream that came to reality through the collective efforts of five(5) young entrepreneurs who came together after their university education to pursue a carrier in Animal Production. It was indeed a tough decision to make but having gone through the hassles of the various rigorous trainings in the unuversity, there was no looking back on their decision.

The fear of failure and the joy of succeeding propelled these young entrepreneurs to forge ahead. Of course, there were various challenges on their part but their very humane lecturers were there to guide them along the whole way.

They first started with 500 day old chicks and as God would have it, the birds did so well that the proceed from the sales of these broilers was enough to restock and venture into egg production.

Every other thing that happened is history as the NGO continued to diversify to reach the height at which it stands today.
